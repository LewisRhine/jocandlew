ABSOLUTELY, ANNOUNCING LOVE KNOCKS TWICE IN BURLINGTON IA!

We are excited to announce the dates that you are able to watch our first short film. We first wanted to announce SHOCK PARTY HORROR MOVIE MARATHON in Burlington, Iowa. If you are a horror fan in the area, we would highly recommend that you come out for a night of vintage horror. BUT ALSO I would recommend that you keep an eye out for their calendar in general. In April, they had a great night of Italian horror, SNAFFTER Dark brought in a lot of horror short films in July, and the month of October has an absolutely STACKED lineup of absolute classics. The Capitol is absolutely worth coming out to.

Burlington’s classic Snake Alley landmark is absolutely nuts and we personally cannot believe YOU can just drive on it. Like there’s absolutely no barrier or tickets, it's a whole road of anxiety as a fun quirk of the town.

Come see LOVE KNOCKS TWICE, and stay tuned for Horrors of the Black Mueseum, Burn Witch Burn, and The Pit and the Pendulum. We hear there’s free coffee and donuts for those who make it long and thank you to Tadd for being so great in programming it!

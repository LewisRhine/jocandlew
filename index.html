12 HOURS OF TERROR @ ROZZ-TOX

We want to talk about and announce a screening of LOVE KNOCKS TWICE that is pretty special to us. Rozz Tox will be hosting us in the 12 Hours of Terror programming, and it is our first creative output that will be shown at a place that is a staple of creating a space for artistry locally and worldwide. We put this announcement later because they update mostly only on their website which we love anything that doesn’t give more attention to the meta machine.

We first visited Rozz Tox on a Friday the 13th screening… in March of 2020 and it has infected us since. It is such a special curated unique place. It is a venue that we are honored to be alongside that regularly hosts movie and philosophy discussions, all sorts of different musical acts from around the world, art shows, amateur vinyl dj sets, garden movies, goofy game shows, a go club, and more! It is a gem to the quad cities, and we are grateful they are extending their stage to a couple of normie knuckleheads.

We still need to coordinate but we plan to make this one *extra special*, we will bring some things to giveaway, Lab puppet will be there, and probably more! This is also the only showing IN PERSON that is FREE AND OPEN! Please come!! October 25th, Rozz Tox in Rock Island, IL. BE THERE AT 6 so you aren’t late when it plays at 6:15pm lol.

Update - 10/7/2025
ABSOLUTELY, ANNOUNCING LOVE KNOCKS TWICE IN BURLINGTON IA!

We are excited to announce the dates that you are able to watch our first short film. We first wanted to announce SHOCK PARTY HORROR MOVIE MARATHON in Burlington, Iowa. If you are a horror fan in the area, we would highly recommend that you come out for a night of vintage horror. BUT ALSO I would recommend that you keep an eye out for their calendar in general. In April, they had a great night of Italian horror, SNAFFTER Dark brought in a lot of horror short films in July, and the month of October has an absolutely STACKED lineup of absolute classics. The Capitol is absolutely worth coming out to.

Burlington’s classic Snake Alley landmark is absolutely nuts and we personally cannot believe YOU can just drive on it. Like there’s absolutely no barrier or tickets, it's a whole road of anxiety as a fun quirk of the town.

Come see LOVE KNOCKS TWICE, and stay tuned for Horrors of the Black Mueseum, Burn Witch Burn, and The Pit and the Pendulum. We hear there’s free coffee and donuts for those who make it long and thank you to Tadd for being so great in programming it!
